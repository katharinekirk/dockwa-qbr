Q2 2026 Company Rocks

For Leadership Review. Five proposed rocks for Q2 execution.

Rock 1: Close the Financing Event

Mike Melillo

Close the financing round by end of Q2, resulting in signed term sheet and funds committed.

Measurable outcome: Signed term sheet with committed capital by June 30, 2026.

Rock 2: VP of Engineering Hire

Katharine Kirk

Advance the VP of Engineering search to a final candidate with an accepted offer or confirmed finalist by end of Q2.

Measurable outcome: Offer accepted or finalist confirmed with decision date by June 30, 2026.

Rock 3: Service Work Orders MVP

Kristen Karpen

Deliver the Service Work Orders MVP to beta with pilot marinas creating, tracking, and billing service work through Dockwa.

Measurable outcome: 3 beta marinas actively billing service work through Dockwa by June 30, 2026.

Rock 4: Transient Self-Service via Rate Management

Tyler Kneisel

Make 95% of the marketplace "rate ready" through Rates v3 UX improvements, analytics instrumentation, and a marina nurturing program.

Measurable outcome: 95% of marketplace marinas have current, valid rates configured.

Rock 5: Enterprise Team & Implementation Playbook

Matt Fradette

Define and operationalize the enterprise customer lifecycle from contract through implementation through ongoing success.

Measurable outcome: Enterprise implementation intake process standardized and deployed for all new enterprise accounts.
