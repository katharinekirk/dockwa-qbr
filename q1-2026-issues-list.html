Product & Engineering 12
1. ThoughtSpot Analytics Dashboards: Booking vs. Served Data Confusion
Safe Harbor analytics kickoff revealed dashboards default to booking metrics when marinas need served metrics for revenue planning. Terminology is misleading ("booking metrics" vs "confirmed reservations"). Served tab layout doesn't match booking tab. No prior-year comparisons on served view. Timeframe filter broken during demo.
Slack #shm, Mar 4 High Product / Safe Harbor
2. Waitlist Data Quality Bug
Reservations that pass through the waitlist at any point remain labeled "waitlisted" permanently in reports, even after confirmation. Skews all reporting downstream. Steph McLaughlin (SH Regional VP) raised this directly. Hannah following up with specific examples.
Slack #shm, Mar 4 High Product / Data Quality
3. RevRec Dates Default to Today Instead of Reservation Dates
When adding line items (electric, trailer, etc.) to a reservation, revenue recognition dates default to today's date rather than the reservation start/end date. Requires manual correction every time. Causes reporting errors for marinas using accrual accounting. Adrian committed to submitting a quick-win product fix; Hannah writing the ticket.
Slack #shm, Mar 4 High Product / Finance
4. Dockwa Chat Email Reply-To Bug (Gmail Domains)
Emails sent from gmail.com addresses fail to forward replies back into Dockwa Chat threads. Issue has persisted since at least November 2025. Blocks communication workflows for marinas relying on Chat for boater correspondence.
Slack #troubleshooting Medium Product / Support
5. Boater Account Linkage / Authentication Failure
Boater accounts are not automatically linked on authentication even when email addresses match. Boaters get stuck in endless login loops. Blocks the boater onboarding experience and reduces activation.
Slack #troubleshooting High Product / Boater Experience
6. Space Group Configuration is a "Black Box"
Tyler called space groups "something of a black box" that are "very challenging to troubleshoot." Boats with no dimensions get routed to waitlist instead of error. Recommendation field shows full/irrelevant space groups in pending reservations, creating scrolling issues. Multiple marinas affected (Brunswick Landing, Port of South Whidbey).
Slack #product_experts, Feb-Mar Medium Product / MMS
7. Rates v2 Limitations and $0 Rate Bug
Rates v2 doesn't support Storage spaces (Dock/Mooring only), no biannual or annual timeframes. $0 rate bug causes boaters to get stuck at the pricing step. Past Rates toggle returns empty results for v2 marinas. View Rates button only appears on post-migration reservations.
Slack #troubleshooting, Mar 2 Medium Product / Pricing
8. Contract Import Custom Rates Defaulting to Group Default
Pope's Island Marina: ~200 contracts with custom prices all defaulted to group default rate on import. Import sheet may have issues or there's a platform bug. CS-1768 filed. Requires manual rate correction per contract if import can't be fixed.
Slack #product_experts, Dec 9 Medium Product / Implementation
